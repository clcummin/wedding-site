Dress Code

Celebrate with us in your most elegant black-tie looks, with a nod to comfort on grass and turf.

Black Tie

We can’t wait to celebrate and dance the night away with you. To make the evening feel as special as it is, please join us in dressing to impress. Let’s make it a stylish night to remember.

Note: The ceremony and celebration will take place outdoors on grass and turf—please choose shoes with that in mind.

Ladies

  • Black floor-length gowns or sophisticated full-length cocktail dresses.
  • Block heels, wedges or dressy flats.

Men

  • Black suits or tuxedos with a crisp dress shirt.
  • Polished dress shoes.
  • A tie or bow tie is encouraged.

Please Avoid

  • Any colors other than black (unless informed by the bride and/or groom).
  • Denim or casual attire.
  • Anything too revealing or sheer.
  • Flip flops or athletic shoes.
  • Party or theme colors such as emerald or green.
  • White outfits – let's keep that shade for the bride.
