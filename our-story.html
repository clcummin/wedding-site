Our Story

We met by chance, but quickly realized we were meant to be together. From our first conversation, our journey has been filled with laughter, adventure, and love.

Introduction

Step into our reimagined love story: a playful comic journey from strangers to soulmates. We met by chance, fell in love by choice—and this flipbook is just a page in our love story.

The Bare Minimum (2015)

2015. My buddy calls, fresh off a chat with his wife, buzzing: ‘Dude, she’s got the job, the degree—the whole package!’ I laugh—what did I say to make him think a résumé equals ‘the one’? Then he drops the bomb: she’s already off the market. Just a name, a face…and I respected that boundary, filed it away…for now.”

God Mode (2018)

Fast‑forward to our annual girls’ reunion in 2018. There I am—single for a year and happily steering clear of blind dates—when, of course, the topic comes up. Sunshine grins and says, “I know the perfect guy.” Before I can roll my eyes, everyone chimes in: “Oh, Chris? He’s fantastic!” Suddenly my friends have built Chris up like some kind of modern-day deity. Me? I’m a die‑hard skeptic. But with that glowing endorsement? How could I resist? Begrudgingly, I admit, “Okay, you’ve convinced me—tell him to hit up my Google+ if he’s interested.” Now, if they’d hyped him up any more, I’d expect lightning bolts and a cape… but hey, you’ve got to see if the legend lives up to the hype!

No Habla Ingles

Karl kicked things off by buzzing Chris Lorraine’s Google+ link—only for Chris to snort, “Google+? No thanks, I need a number!” So Sunshine slipped him Lorraine’s digits behind the scenes. A few days later Chris called, only to be met with a cheerful “¡Hola! No habla inglés!” from a coworker screening her calls. Undeterred, he fired off a text explaining who he was and apologizing for the surprise. Lorraine, swamped at the office and wary of unknown numbers, sheepishly admitted the prank—and that tiny mix‑up paved the way for a six‑hour, laughter‑filled midnight chat.

The First Date

On March 23, 2019, Chris jetted into LA—armed only with a hotel key and zero LA intel to woo Lorraine. His first “date night” gamble landed him at a breakfast buffet (he’d secretly hoped for a chic rooftop brunch), where he nervously perched at the bar. Through the mirrored backdrop, he spotted her: a statuesque beauty in long black silk, gliding in like a movie star. After a shy hug, the awkward first bites gave way to electric chemistry: they chased pool balls at a dive bar, cheered on the Rangers from a luxe suite, and capped the night over dollar‑oyster slurps at a cozy Korean joint. By the time they parted, it was clear: this was just the opening scene of something extraordinary.

The Proposal

On March 7, 2024, a contemplative Chris found himself swapping stories with his favorite bartender—who quipped, “Either shit or get off the toilet” and it hit him: time to stop talking and start doing. After his family blessed the idea (“As long as you’re happy”), he whisked Lorraine to a jewelry store—where what he’d budgeted for fifteen minutes stretched into a full hour of ring‑shopping indecision. Many months later, ring in pocket, he led her back to the Marin Headlands—exactly where he’d first asked her to be his girlfriend four years earlier—rolled out a red carpet, changed into his best suit on the hillside, and dropped to one knee. Lorraine gave a joyful “Yes,” and the next day they toasted under a surprise celebration.
