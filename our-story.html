When Cummings Meets Galano

Introduction

Step into our reimagined love story: a playful comic journey from strangers to soulmates. We met by chance, fell in love by choice—and this flipbook is just a page in our love story.

The Bare Minimum (2015)

2015. My buddy calls, fresh off a chat with his wife, buzzing: ‘Dude, she’s got the job, the degree—the whole package!’ I laugh—what did I say to make him think a résumé equals ‘the one’? Then he drops the bomb: she’s already off the market. Just a name, a face…and I respected that boundary, filed it away…for now.”

God Mode (2018)

Fast‑forward to our annual girls’ reunion in 2018. There I am—single for a year and happily steering clear of blind dates—when, of course, the topic comes up. Sunshine grins and says, “I know the perfect guy.” Before I can roll my eyes, everyone chimes in: “Oh, Chris? He’s fantastic!” Suddenly my friends have built Chris up like some kind of modern-day deity. Me? I’m a die‑hard skeptic. But with that glowing endorsement? How could I resist? Begrudgingly, I admit, “Okay, you’ve convinced me—tell him to hit up my Google+ if he’s interested.” Now, if they’d hyped him up any more, I’d expect lightning bolts and a cape… but hey, you’ve got to see if the legend lives up to the hype!
