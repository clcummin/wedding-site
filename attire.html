Guest Attire

Dress Code: Elegant Sexy Chic

We're excited to celebrate and dance with everyone, and kindly ask that you align with our dress code and suggested color palette.

Color Palette

Suggested Attire

Ladies

  • Floor-length gowns or sophisticated cocktail dresses.
  • Jewel tones, black, or metallics are welcome.
  • Comfortable block heels or dressy flats.

Men

  • Dark suits or tuxedos with a crisp dress shirt.
  • Polished dress shoes.
  • A tie or bow tie is encouraged.

Please Avoid

  • Denim or casual attire.
  • Anything too revealing or sheer.
  • Flip flops or athletic shoes.
  • Party or theme colors such as emerald or green.
  • White outfits – let's keep that shade for the bride.
