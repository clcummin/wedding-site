Guest Attire

Elegant. Classy. Chic.

We can't wait to celebrate and dance the night away with you!

To help make the evening feel as special as it is, we kindly ask that you join us in dressing to impress. Please align with our suggested color palette—think chic, elegant, and elevated neutrals. Let’s make it a stylish night to remember!

Note: The ceremony and celebration will take place outdoors on grass and turf, so please keep that in mind when selecting your attire—especially your shoes.

Suggested Attire

Ladies

  • Floor-length gowns or sophisticated full-length cocktail dresses.
  • Comfortable block heels or dressy flats.

Men

  • Dark suits or tuxedos with a crisp dress shirt.
  • Polished dress shoes.
  • A tie or bow tie is encouraged.

Please Avoid

  • Denim or casual attire.
  • Anything too revealing or sheer.
  • Flip flops or athletic shoes.
  • Party or theme colors such as emerald or green.
  • White outfits – let's keep that shade for the bride.
