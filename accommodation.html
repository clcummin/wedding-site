Accommodations

We are thrilled to invite you to join us for our wedding weekend at the beautiful Chalet View Lodge, a premier boutique resort nestled in the scenic town of Portola, CA. Just a 45‑minute drive north of Truckee and 50 miles from the Reno‑Tahoe International Airport. Chalet View Lodge provides the comfort of a relaxed, no‑curfew environment. It's the essence of mountain luxury, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the natural world, and provides a peaceful sanctuary amid the wilderness. It also serves as the perfect launchpad for adventures, from trekking through forest trails to enjoying the myriad of activities onsite.

How to Book

  1. Click HERE to visit our custom booking page
  2. Select your arrival and departure dates from the calendar
  3. Press “Check Availability”
  4. Select the room type most suitable for your party, and press “View Rates”
  5. Under Available Offers, press “Reserve”
  6. Confirm your reservation by filling out the form on the next page

Please do not call the hotel to reserve your room(s) for your stay—the Front Desk will be unable to assist you. If you need help, email help@chaletview.com.

Hotel Details

To ensure an intimate and exclusive experience, we have reserved all 52 uniquely styled rooms at the property. These rooms will be held for our wedding party until 60 days prior to our event. We encourage you to book as soon as possible to secure your preferred room type.

Check‑in time is 4:00 PM and check‑out time is 11:00 AM. Chalet View Lodge is a small boutique hotel and cannot accommodate early check‑ins or late check‑outs. Please plan accordingly.

Overflow Accommodations

Unfortunately, not all of our guests will be able to stay at the lodge, so please make your reservations early to get the room you love! Once our block fills, we will post recommended nearby hotels here.

Airport & Car Rentals

The easiest airport to fly into is Reno‑Tahoe International Airport, about a 50‑minute drive. We recommend renting a car for the weekend; consider coordinating with friends to share a vehicle. See Rental Car Options .

Directions from Reno‑Tahoe Airport

  1. Head west, take the left ramp onto I‑580 North.
  2. Keep right to merge onto US‑395 North toward Susanville.
  3. After ~25 miles, take exit 8 at Hallelujah Junction for CA‑70 toward Portola/Quincy.
  4. Turn left at the stop sign onto CA‑70 West and continue 27 miles.
  5. Chalet View Lodge will be on your left past Portola, CA.
