Accommodations

We are thrilled to invite you to join us for our wedding weekend at the beautiful Chalet View Lodge, a premier boutique resort nestled in the scenic town of Portola, CA— just a 45‑minute drive north of Truckee and 50 miles from the Reno‑Tahoe International Airport.

Chalet View Lodge provides the comfort of a relaxed, no‑curfew environment. It's the essence of mountain luxury, inviting guests to immerse themselves in the natural world, and serves as the perfect launchpad for adventures—from forest trails to onsite activities.

How to Book Your Room

  1. Visit our custom booking page by clicking HERE .
  2. Select your arrival and departure dates.
  3. Click “Check Availability.”
  4. Choose your room type and press “View Rates.”
  5. Under Available Offers, press “Reserve.”
  6. Complete the reservation form on the next page.

Important: Do not call the hotel to book—it cannot process your block‑rate code. For help, email help@chaletview.com.

Hotel Details

We’ve reserved all 52 uniquely styled rooms exclusively for our group until 60 days before the wedding. Book early to secure your preferred room.

Check‑in: 4:00 PM • Check‑out: 11:00 AM
Chalet View Lodge cannot accommodate early check‑ins or late check‑outs.

Airport & Car Rentals

Reno‑Tahoe International Airport is a 50‑minute drive away. We recommend renting a car— consider coordinating rides with friends. Rental Car Info .

Directions from Reno‑Tahoe Airport

  1. Take the left ramp onto I‑580 N.
  2. Merge onto US‑395 N toward Susanville.
  3. After ~25 mi, exit 8 at Hallelujah Junction for CA‑70 toward Portola/Quincy.
  4. Turn left onto CA‑70 W and continue 27 mi.
  5. Chalet View Lodge will appear on your left past Portola, CA.
