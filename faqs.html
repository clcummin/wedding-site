Frequently Asked Questions

How can I modify or cancel my reservation?

The best way to modify or cancel your reservation is following the steps here, or emailing heather@chaletview.com directly.

Are Kids Welcome?

Please refer to your invitation—every named guest is listed there. The celebration is at a resort where children are welcome to stay and enjoy the onsite activities. However, the ceremony and reception are reserved for the guests named on your invitation and their plus ones.

CVL Cancelation Policy

Upon booking your hotel room, Chalet View Lodge will require a deposit of one night plus taxes and fees. If you are staying for multiple nights, they will charge the balance for the remaining night(s) upon check-out. If you need to cancel your reservation for any reason, please do so at least 72 hours before you are scheduled to check-in, and the lodge will issue a 100% refund of your deposit. If you cancel within the 72 hour window, unfortunately you forfeit your deposit.

Where will the ceremony and reception be held?

For April-May + October-November Weddings: Our ceremony & cocktail hour will be held outside. Our reception will be in a heated tent.
For June-September Weddings: Our ceremony & reception will be held outside!

Where can I park?

There is plenty of free parking at Chalet View.

Can I invite a plus one?

Please refer to your invitation if there’s an additional guest invited.

What shoes should I wear?

Our celebration is outdoors on grass, turf, and gravel, so please avoid stilettos, sandals, and flats. Opt for elegant, classy footwear like block heels or wedges that can handle the terrain—see our attire page for more inspiration. Bringing a spare pair of flats for dancing the night away is totally acceptable!
