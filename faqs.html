Frequently Asked Questions

A quick guide to the most common questions so you can arrive relaxed, dressed to impress, and ready to celebrate.

How can I modify or cancel my reservation?

The smoothest way is to follow the booking steps on our travel page or email heather@chaletview.com directly—she’ll help adjust your stay.

Are Kids Welcome?

Please refer to your RSVP—everyone invited is listed there. The resort welcomes children to enjoy the weekend, but the ceremony and reception are reserved for the guests named on your RSVP and their plus ones.

CVL Cancellation Policy

Chalet View Lodge requires a one-night deposit (plus taxes and fees) at booking. The remaining balance is due at checkout. If you need to cancel, please do so at least 72 hours before arrival for a full refund of your deposit; inside that window, the deposit is forfeited.

Where will the ceremony and reception be held?

Both the ceremony and reception will be outdoors on the lawn at Chalet View Lodge.

Where can I park?

There is plenty of free parking at Chalet View.

Can I invite a plus one?

Please refer to your rsvp if there’s an additional guest invited.

What shoes should I wear?

Our celebration is outdoors on grass, turf, and gravel. Opt for elegant, sturdy footwear like block heels or wedges—see our dress code page for inspiration. Bringing a pair of flats for dancing is encouraged.

What is a money dance and money toss?

Bring money to participate. Make sure you have singles to give to the bride to dance, and then have money to toss—you just might win some.
