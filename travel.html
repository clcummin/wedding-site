Arriving in Portola, CA

Travel

Everything you need to get to Chalet View Lodge and settle in for the weekend.

Airport

Reno‑Tahoe International Airport

Reno‑Tahoe International Airport is a 50‑minute drive away. We recommend renting a car—consider coordinating rides with friends. Rental Car Info .

Drive

Directions from Reno‑Tahoe Airport

  1. Take the left ramp onto I‑580 N.
  2. Merge onto US‑395 N toward Susanville.
  3. After ~25 mi, exit 8 at Hallelujah Junction for CA‑70 toward Portola/Quincy.
  4. Turn left onto CA‑70 W and continue 27 mi.
  5. Chalet View Lodge will appear on your left past Portola, CA.
Stay

Accommodations

We’ve reserved all 52 uniquely styled rooms at Chalet View Lodge until 60 days before the wedding. It’s our weekend HQ with a relaxed, no‑curfew vibe—book early to claim your favorite space.

Check‑in: 4:00 PM · Check‑out: 11:00 AM. The resort isn’t able to accommodate early check‑ins or late check‑outs.

Transport

Vans from Stockton & SMF

Flying into Stockton or Sacramento (SMF)? We can organize van shares from either airport so you arrive with the crew.

Reach out to Lorraine or Chris at least a week before you land, and we’ll coordinate the van details with you.

Relax

Enjoy the Chalet

The lodge is reserved for us the entire weekend. Enjoy all the spaces available; swimming pool, fire pits, play-ground, golf greens and much much more! A wedding for us a mini-vacation for you. Also, check out the town! A small town vibe with a few hidden gems!

Bookings

Book your room

  1. Visit our custom booking page by clicking HERE .
  2. Select your arrival and departure dates.
  3. Click “Check Availability.”
  4. Choose your room type and press “View Rates.”
  5. Under Available Offers, press “Reserve.”
  6. Complete the reservation form on the next page.

Important: Do not call the hotel to book—it cannot process your block‑rate code. For help, email help@chaletview.com.
